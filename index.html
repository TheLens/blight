Has this blight been fixed?

This map shows properties that appeared on one of four BlightStat databases as of January.

It includes properties remediated in one of four ways:

  • Demolition
  • Sold by the New Orleans Redevelopment Authority
  • Seized by the city and sold by the Orleans Parish Sheriff’s Office
  • Found to be in compliance with city code at a code enforcement hearing

For sales, the date shown is when the sale occurred. For demolitions, it's the date the demolition was marked as completed. For compliance, it's the date that a hearing officer found the property to be in line with city code.

To investigate a property further, go to any of the above databases or the city’s OneStop App, which shows code violations and city permits.
